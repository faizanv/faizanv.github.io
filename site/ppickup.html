Article From StrapHQ About The App

Pebble Pickup is a Pebble Smartwatch app that won the Strap sponsor prize at MHacks V at the University of Michigan. It gives the user pickup lines to use at the flick of wrist. The pickup lines are all crowd sourced from Twitter using #PebblePickup and pulled directly into the app.
